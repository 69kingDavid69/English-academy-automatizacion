Language Academy - Certification Requirements

1. Certification Overview

Students receive a certificate after completing each level.

2. Requirements

To obtain a certificate, students must:

- Complete the full course duration
- Attend at least 80% of classes
- Pass the final exam with a minimum score of 70%

3. Final Exam

- Includes speaking, listening, reading, and writing
- Conducted in the last week of the course

4. Types of Certificates

- Completion Certificate (basic requirement met)
- Excellence Certificate (score above 90%)

5. Validity

- Certificates are valid as proof of course completion
- Not an official international certification

6. Additional Certifications

The academy offers preparation courses for:
- IELTS
- TOEFL

These require separate enrollment.
