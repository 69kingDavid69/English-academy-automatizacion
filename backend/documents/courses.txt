Language Academy - Courses, Schedules, and Levels

1. Course Levels

The academy follows the CEFR standard:

- A1: Beginner
- A2: Elementary
- B1: Intermediate
- B2: Upper Intermediate
- C1: Advanced
- C2: Proficiency

2. Course Modalities

- Online (live classes via video)
- In-person (at academy facilities)
- Hybrid (combination of both)

3. Class Schedules

Weekday Schedule:
- Morning: 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM
- Afternoon: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
- Evening: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Weekend Schedule:
- Saturday: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM

4. Duration

- Each level lasts 8 weeks
- Classes are held 3 times per week

5. Placement Test

- Required for non-beginner students
- Conducted online
- Determines appropriate level

6. Attendance Policy

- Minimum 80% attendance required
- Missing more than 20% may result in failing the level
