Language Academy - Frequently Asked Questions

1. What languages do you offer?
We currently offer English courses only.

2. Do I need prior knowledge?
No. Beginners can start at level A1.

3. How do I know my level?
You can take a placement test before enrolling.

4. Can I change my schedule?
Yes, subject to availability.

5. Are classes recorded?
Yes, online classes are recorded and shared with students.

6. What happens if I miss a class?
You can access the recording or request support from the instructor.

7. Do you offer certificates?
Yes, after completing the course and passing the final exam.

8. Can I get a refund?
Refunds are only available within the first 5 days after enrollment.

9. Do you offer group discounts?
Yes, groups of 3 or more receive a 15% discount.

10. How long does each level take?
Each level lasts 8 weeks.

11. Are there private classes?
Yes, private classes are available at an hourly rate.

12. Do you prepare for international exams?
Yes, we offer preparation courses for IELTS and TOEFL.
