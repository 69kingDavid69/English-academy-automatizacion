Language Academy - Pricing and Enrollment Information

1. General Pricing

The academy offers flexible pricing plans depending on the course level and modality.

- Basic Level (A1 - A2): $120 USD per month
- Intermediate Level (B1 - B2): $150 USD per month
- Advanced Level (C1 - C2): $180 USD per month

Private classes:
- $25 USD per hour

Discounts:
- 10% discount for full payment of 3 months
- 15% discount for groups of 3 or more students

2. Enrollment Process

To enroll in a course, students must follow these steps:

1. Fill out the registration form (online or in person)
2. Take a placement test (if not a beginner)
3. Choose schedule and modality
4. Complete payment
5. Receive confirmation email

3. Payment Methods

- Credit/Debit Card
- Bank Transfer
- Online Payment Platforms

4. Refund Policy

- Refunds are available within the first 5 days after enrollment
- No refunds after classes have started

5. Important Notes

- Enrollment is confirmed only after payment
- Students must attend at least 80% of classes to complete a level
